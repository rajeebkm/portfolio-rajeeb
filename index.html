Want to know more about "Rajeeb Kumar Malik"

His areas are mainly focuses on leading edge Blockchain Technology, Decentralised Finance, Cryptocurrency, Non-Fungible Tokens NFT, ERC Standards, Blockchain Protocols, Metaverse, NFT Games, EVM Blockchain, Solana Blockchain etc.
